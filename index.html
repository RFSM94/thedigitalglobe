The Digital Globe

The world is a stage and the web has made it bigger. Many narratives happen within it, good and bad. It is humans who can shuffle them and contribute for what the digital globe became and will become.

Dirty Data

Dirty data, are inaccurate, incomplete or inconsistent data, especially in a computer system or database. They’re also the base of algorithmic prediction models, which currently shape the digital and real world.

Democratic Bits

The creation of the Personal Computer and the World Wide Web were seen as promised lands of enhancement for the future of humanity, thanks to its infinite potencial of creating, organizing and sharing ideas.
